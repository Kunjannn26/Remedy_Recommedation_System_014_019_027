Home Remedies for Common Diseases

Diabetes

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. Avoid sugary drinks and processed foods. Get regular exercise. Monitor your blood sugar levels regularly.

Cancer

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. Avoid smoking and exposure to tobacco smoke. Limit alcohol consumption. Get regular exercise.

Cardiovascular

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. Limit saturated and trans fats in your diet. Get regular exercise. Avoid smoking and exposure to tobacco smoke. Monitor your blood pressure and cholesterol levels regularly.

Alzheimer

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. Engage in regular mental stimulation, such as reading or puzzles. Stay socially active. Get regular exercise.

Home Remedies for Common Ailments

Headache

Drink plenty of water, apply a cold compress to your forehead, or try an over-the-counter pain reliever such as ibuprofen or acetaminophen.

Fever

Rest and drink plenty of fluids. You can also try over-the-counter fever reducers such as acetaminophen or ibuprofen.

Cough

Sip warm water, honey and lemon tea, or a mixture of honey and ginger juice. You can also try over-the-counter cough medicines.

Sore Throat

Gargle with warm salt water, drink plenty of fluids, or try over-the-counter throat lozenges or sprays.

Fatigue

Get plenty of rest, eat a balanced diet, and exercise regularly. Limit caffeine and alcohol intake, and try relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.

Muscle Aches

Apply a warm compress to the affected area, take over-the-counter pain relievers such as ibuprofen or acetaminophen, or try a massage.

Runny Nose

Stay hydrated, use a saline nasal spray or rinse, or try over-the-counter decongestants.

Sneezing

Avoid triggers such as pollen, dust, or pet dander, and take over-the-counter antihistamines or decongestants.

Nausea

Eat small, frequent meals, avoid spicy or greasy foods, and try over-the-counter antacids or anti-nausea medications.

Vomiting

Drink clear liquids, eat bland foods such as crackers or toast, and try over-the-counter anti-nausea medications.
