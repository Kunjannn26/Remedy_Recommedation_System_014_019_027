Lab Report Tests

Diabetes

{% comment %}

If you have been diagnosed with diabetes, your doctor may order the following lab report tests:

{% endcomment %}
Test Name Description
Fasting blood glucose test Measures your blood glucose level after you have fasted for at least eight hours
Random blood glucose test Measures your blood glucose level at any time of the day, regardless of when you last ate
Oral glucose tolerance test Measures your blood glucose level before and two hours after you drink a sugary drink
Hemoglobin A1C test Measures your average blood glucose level over the past two to three months
Lipid profile Measures your cholesterol and triglyceride levels
Kidney function tests Measures your kidney function, which can be affected by diabetes

High Blood Pressure

{% comment %}

If you have been diagnosed with diabetes, your doctor may order the following lab report tests:

{% endcomment %}
Test Name Description
Blood Pressure Test Measures the force of blood against the walls of the arteries. A reading of 130/80 mm Hg or higher indicates high blood pressure.
Urine Test Checks for protein in the urine, which can be a sign of kidney damage from high blood pressure.
